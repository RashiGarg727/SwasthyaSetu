🏥 SwasthyaSetu – Personal Health Record Aggregator & AI Summary Tool 🇮🇳
📌 Overview

SwasthyaSetu is a digital health platform designed to solve a major problem in India — fragmented and paper-based medical records.

Patients often carry physical files to consultations, leading to:

Loss of records
Incomplete medical history
Wasted consultation time

👉 This platform allows users to store, manage, and share health records digitally, and generates an AI-powered pre-consultation summary for doctors.
