SwasthyaSetu – Personal Health Record Aggregator & AI Summary Tool
Overview

SwasthyaSetu is a digital health platform designed to address a widespread issue in India’s healthcare system: fragmented and paper-based medical records. Patients often carry physical reports and prescriptions to every consultation, which can lead to loss of records, incomplete medical history, and inefficient use of consultation time. This project provides a solution by enabling patients to store their health records digitally and generate a structured health summary that can be easily shared with doctors.

Problem Statement

A significant portion of healthcare institutions in India still rely on paper-based or hybrid systems for maintaining patient records. This results in poor accessibility, lack of continuity in care, and increased chances of medical errors. Patients do not have ownership over their health data, and doctors often spend valuable time reconstructing medical history during consultations. There is a clear need for a unified, patient-centric platform that organizes and presents medical data efficiently.

Solution

SwasthyaSetu provides a centralized platform where users can upload and manage their medical reports, prescriptions, and diagnostic records. The system processes this data and generates a structured, easy-to-understand summary of the patient’s health profile. This summary includes key information such as medical conditions, medications, allergies, and important observations, allowing doctors to quickly grasp the patient’s history before consultation.

Key Features

The platform allows users to upload medical documents in formats such as PDF and images and view them in a single dashboard. It generates an AI-based health summary that organizes patient data into meaningful categories. The system also highlights important insights such as recurring conditions and potential health risks. Additionally, users can share their health data through multiple channels, including downloadable summaries, secure links, email, and messaging platforms. Each user is assigned a unique Health ID, making the system compatible with future integration into national health infrastructures.

Technology Stack

The project is built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for the frontend. It uses browser-based file handling for uploading and managing documents. AI-based summary generation is currently simulated, with provisions for integrating OCR tools like Tesseract.js and language models such as OpenAI or Gemini APIs for real-world implementation.

Working

Users upload their medical reports through the platform interface. The system processes these documents and extracts relevant information, either through simulated logic or planned OCR integration. This extracted data is then passed to an AI module that generates a structured health summary. The final summary can be downloaded or shared with a doctor prior to consultation.

Impact

SwasthyaSetu improves the efficiency of healthcare delivery by reducing the time doctors spend understanding patient history. It minimizes the risk of lost or incomplete records and enhances the accuracy of diagnosis by providing structured and accessible information. The platform supports India’s transition towards digital healthcare and aligns with initiatives like the Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission.

Future Scope

Future enhancements include full integration with ABHA ID under the Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission, implementation of real OCR and NLP-based data extraction, cloud-based storage for persistent records, and development of a dedicated doctor interface. Advanced analytics and predictive health insights can also be incorporated to further improve patient care.

Disclaimer

This project is a prototype developed for demonstration purposes. The AI-generated summaries are simulated and should not be used for actual medical decision-making.
